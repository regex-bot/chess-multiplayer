About Chess Online

Chess Online is a free, web-based chess platform that brings the classic game to your browser. Whether you want to play a quick game with a friend sitting next to you or challenge someone across the globe, we've got you covered.

Features

  • Full FIDE Rules: All standard chess rules including castling, en passant, pawn promotion, check, checkmate, and stalemate.
  • Local Multiplayer: Play on the same device with a friend.
  • Online Multiplayer: Create or join rooms using simple room codes.
  • Flexible Time Controls: Choose from multiple time formats (3, 5, 10, 15, 30 minutes) or play without a clock.
  • Live Chat: Communicate with your opponent during online games.
  • Move History: Track all moves with standard chess notation.
  • Draw & Rematch: Offer draws and request rematches seamlessly.

How to Play

Click "Play Now" to get started. Choose between local or online multiplayer, select your preferred time control, and start playing! For online games, simply create a room and share the code with your friend, or join an existing room.
